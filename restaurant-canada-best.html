Our Story
4.8 Google Rating

A Hidden Gem
in Parc-Extension

Run by a husband and wife team with a deep passion for the flavours of Malaysia and Sri Lanka, Restaurant Canada Best is a neighbourhood treasure. Every dish is made fresh, with carefully sourced spices and ingredients that bring the warmth of Southeast Asia to Montréal.

Our Nasi Lemak — coconut-rice, sambal, ikan bilis, crispy chicken, egg and peanuts — is what keeps people coming back. But don't sleep on the Rendang Ayam, the Chicken 65, or our famous Saturday Special.
