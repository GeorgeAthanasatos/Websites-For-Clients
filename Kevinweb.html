About Kevin

A Calgarian since grade two, and a tree person for longer than that.

Kevin grew up exploring the Rocky Mountain front and spending summers on a mixed farm in central Saskatchewan — planted by his great-grandfather in 1928, still holding a grove of Scots pine.

"Disturb the roots, look for die-back above. It's one whole tree in balance — not five separate parts."

That principle now guides every consultation: no upselling, no guessing — just decades of hands-on pattern recognition applied to your yard's actual problem.
